Practice Problem 2.10
Inplace swap by using XOR.

void inplace_swap(int *x, int *y) {
  *y = *x ^ *y;
  *x = *x ^ *y;
  *y = *x ^ *y;
}

  Step        *x        *y
  ------------------------------------
  Initially    a         b
     Step 1    a       a^b
     Step 2    a^(a^b) a^b
     Step 3    a^(a^b) (a^(a^b))^(a^b)
     Result    a^a^b   a^a^b^a^b
     Equals    b       a
